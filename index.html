The process

How the graveyard works

01
Bury your project
Submit your dead project — name, description, tech stack, and why it died. Choose to list it for free adoption, for sale, or just to memorialize it.
02
Others discover it
Developers, indie hackers, and builders browse the graveyard looking for projects worth resurrecting. Your project gets a second audience.
03
Get adopted or sold
Someone falls in love with your idea. They adopt it for free or pay you for the codebase, domain, and learnings. We take 5% on sales.
04
It lives again
The new owner relaunches your project. It gets a "Resurrected" badge on the site. Your idea finally gets the ending it deserved.
